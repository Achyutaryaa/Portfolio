About

Hello! I'm Achyutarya Raaz, a passionate Frontend Web Developer with a knack for creating dynamic and responsive web applications. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I strive to build user-friendly and visually appealing websites. My journey in web development started with a curiosity for how websites work, which quickly turned into a full-fledged passion. Over the years, I have honed my skills and worked on various projects that showcase my ability to turn ideas into reality. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading tech blogs, or contributing to open-source projects. I believe in continuous learning and am always looking for opportunities to grow and improve my skill set. Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see some of the projects I've worked on. If you have any questions or would like to collaborate, don't hesitate to reach out!
